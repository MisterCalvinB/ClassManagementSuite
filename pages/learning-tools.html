📖 How It Works

A complete guide — filters, games, tools, and team mode.

🎛 Filters
Level, Theme, Type — use these filters to narrow the word pool before starting any game. Select multiple values from any picker; only words matching all active filters will appear.
Filters carry over — when you navigate from game to game, your filter selections stay in place. Use Reset to clear everything back to "All".
Multiple themes per word — a word can belong to several themes (e.g. "Emotions, Character"). It will appear whenever any of its themes is selected.
📖 Vocabulary Games
🧩 Definition Match
Flip cards to pair each word with its correct definition. Click a word card, then its matching definition card. All pairs must be found to win.
😵 Hangman
Guess the hidden word letter by letter. Choose your number of allowed mistakes (4–12). Use 💡 Clue to reveal the definition.
🔠 Scrambled Word
The word's letters are shuffled. Click letters to build your answer, use 💡 Clue for a letter hint, then submit.
🔍 Word Quest
A definition is shown. Unlock up to 4 progressive clues (synonyms, letter hints…) to narrow it down, then type your guess.
🔉 Phonetic Guess
An IPA phonetic transcription is shown. Identify the word it represents — pick from 4 choices or type the answer directly.
🔗 Synonyms & Antonyms
A word is shown — find its synonym or antonym. Toggle between Synonym, Antonym, or Both modes. Multiple choice or free typing.
📐 Grammar Games
✍️ Sentence Builder
Reconstruct a scrambled sentence by clicking words in the correct order. The target structure is shown; use 💡 Clue to reveal the next word.
🕳️ Gap Fill
A sentence has one word removed. Type the missing word. Filter by grammar type (Relatives, Conditionals…) for targeted practice.
🔴 Find the Error
Spot the grammatical mistake in a sentence. Two modes: Spot (choose correct vs erroneous) or Rewrite (correct the sentence yourself).
📝 Dictation
A text is read aloud. Type what you hear, word by word. Choose Easy (10% words hidden), Medium (50%), or Hard (90%) difficulty.
📚 Tools
📐 Grammar Practice
A reference library of grammar rules — formulas, signals words, common mistakes, and tips. Filter by category or level.
🎴 Flash Cards
Hover over a card to flip it and reveal the French translation and definition. Great for passive review.
📜 Quote Analyser
A literary quote is displayed. Guess its theme and keywords; unlock clues one by one or request a full analysis.
📋 View All Words
Browse the entire vocabulary bank filtered by your current settings. Click any word for full details. Export to CSV or TXT.
🌐 Word Constellation
Visual word-map showing connections between words by theme and meaning. Zoom, drag, and explore relationships.
➕ Add Words to DB
Add custom vocabulary, quotes, gap-fill sentences, and error pairs. Saved in your browser; download an updated DB file to make them permanent.
👥 Team Mode
Add teams on the home screen — give each a name and colour. Teams take turns across all games; the current team's colour highlights the game panel.
Scoring — a correct answer awards points to the active team. Use Reset Scores to zero everything, or Clear Teams to remove all teams.
⏱ Timer
Most games have a ⏱ Timer button in the top-right. Click it to set a countdown; it runs independently of the game so you can use it for timed rounds or team challenges.
